## B2: Constraint as Information (Next)

**Claim:** Shannon's information theory and geometric constraint are the same thing in different notation. A constraint that eliminates possibilities is informationally equivalent to bits received. Frozen type embeddings implement this: they don't make hallucination *unlikely* — they make certain outputs *geometrically impossible*.

**Bochkov citation required here:** "representational interference" is the noise floor that frozen embeddings eliminate. Our framing explains *why* this works at the information-theoretic level.
