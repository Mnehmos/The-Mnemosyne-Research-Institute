## Abstract Summary

The proposal targets TA1: generating formally verified mathematical training data at scale. ChatDB produces 3–4 million typed reward signals per IMO corpus pass, each step formally verified by SymPy/Lean before inclusion. This addresses DARPA's core problem: LLMs trained on unverified mathematical reasoning inherit hallucination from that data.

**Key differentiator from competing proposals:** Co-design with ANLU means the training data structure matches the model architecture geometrically. This is not a data augmentation strategy — it is a typed co-design system.
