About

The Science of Remembering

Named for Mnemosyne — the Greek Titaness of memory and mother of the Muses — our institute is dedicated to understanding how intelligent systems encode, retrieve, and reason over knowledge.

🧠

Memory Architecture

We study how AI systems can maintain persistent, queryable memory across sessions — moving beyond the context window.

🔗

Agent Coordination

Building protocols and tools for multi-agent systems that collaborate through shared external state rather than fragile in-context communication.

📊

Knowledge Systems

Developing pipelines for indexing, embedding, and retrieving knowledge at scale — from RAG to structured reasoning engines.

🔓

Open Research

Our findings, tools, and frameworks are published openly. We believe the cognitive infrastructure of the future should be shared.
